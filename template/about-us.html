About us

OITC Co., Ltd is a leading Vietnamese technology company that has been operating since 2001. Specializing in providing cutting-edge information technology products and comprehensive IT solutions, the company has earned a strong reputation in the industry. OITC offers a wide variety of products, including motherboards, servers, blade servers, chassis, and other essential IT accessories. Its product offerings are designed to meet the diverse needs of businesses, ranging from small enterprises to large corporations, looking for reliable and high-performance IT infrastructure. With a commitment to excellence, OITC focuses on delivering superior products and services that help businesses optimize their IT operations. The company's headquarters are located in Hanoi, Vietnam, and it serves as a trusted partner for companies seeking advanced technological solutions to enhance productivity and streamline their operations. OITC's long-standing presence in the industry is a testament to its expertise and dedication to providing top-quality IT solutions to its clients.

About my engineer team

OITC Co., Ltd prides itself on its highly skilled and experienced team of engineers who are dedicated to delivering innovative IT solutions. The company's engineering team is made up of professionals with deep expertise in various areas of information technology, including hardware, software, networking, and system integration. These engineers are committed to understanding the unique needs of each client and designing customized solutions that ensure the highest levels of performance, security, and efficiency. By staying at the forefront of technological advancements, OITC's engineers continuously enhance their skills and knowledge, allowing the company to offer cutting-edge solutions that keep businesses ahead in a fast-paced, technology-driven world. The team's collaborative approach and attention to detail ensure that OITC remains a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable and effective IT infrastructure.
