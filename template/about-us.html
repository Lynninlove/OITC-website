About us

OITC Co., Ltd, established in 2001, is a leading Vietnamese technology company. Specializing in cutting-edge information technology products and comprehensive IT solutions, the company has built a strong reputation in the industry. OITC offers a wide variety of solutions, including storage systems, data protection systems, servers, observability systems, and other essential IT solutions. These offerings are designed to meet the diverse needs of businesses, from small enterprises to large corporations, seeking reliable and high-performance IT infrastructure. Notably, OITC is emerging as a leading provider of IT solutions and services specifically for storage and data protection in Vietnam. With a commitment to excellence, OITC focuses on delivering superior products and services that help businesses optimize their IT operations. Headquartered in Hanoi, Vietnam, the company serves as a trusted partner for organizations seeking advanced technological solutions to enhance productivity and streamline operations. OITC's long- standing industry presence is a testament to its expertise and dedication to providing top- quality IT solutions to its customers.

About my engineer team

At the core of OITC's technical strength is our team of highly experienced engineers and professional technicians. They possess many years of specialized expertise in consulting on, designing, and implementing robust storage systems and comprehensive data protection solutions. This practical knowledge is formally recognized through numerous certifications awarded directly by leading technology manufacturers in these critical areas. Furthermore, our team combines this technical proficiency with an in-depth understanding of Vietnam's specific ICT infrastructure and extensive, hands-on experience in successfully deploying complex ICT projects across the country. This unique blend of certified expertise and local insight ensures we deliver effective, reliable, and tailored solutions that meet the demanding needs of our customers.
